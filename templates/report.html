3. Methodology

This audit follows the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0 structured workflow:

  1. Asset Inventory: All information assets were catalogued with CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) ratings to calculate asset criticality scores.
  2. Threat & Vulnerability Identification: OWASP and CWE vulnerabilities were mapped to each asset, with automated likelihood and impact scoring.
  3. Risk Assessment: Risk scores were calculated using the formula: Risk = Likelihood × Impact, classified as Low (1–4), Medium (5–9), High (10–15), or Critical (16–25).
  4. Security Control Audit: Controls were assessed against the NIST CSF core functions using a four-point scale: Compliant, Partially Compliant, Non Compliant, and Not Applicable.
  5. Compliance Scoring: Compliance % = (Compliant Controls ÷ Total Assessed Controls) × 100.
  6. Findings Generation: Audit findings were systematically generated from non-compliant controls and high-risk vulnerabilities.
